About Me

This is What I Am!

My name is Varun Shrivastava and I’m a web developer. If you are a small business owner who is feeling overwhelmed by ALL the different pieces of technology you need to run your small business, you are about to loooove me! That’s because I can provide extension to your business in very short amount of time.


Want to know more about me? Here’s a little story about my background and how I got into this whole mess! I was raised in an innovative family, my father is a hardware engineer and runs a cable business along with my mother. My mother also takes care of the house. I believe my first business was selling my services in return of my work (bringing bread from the store), though my family was my only customers. My interest for computers grew when I was a child, and I was lucky enough to be exposed early. I still remember the first operating system that I worked on was Microsoft's Windows 98 and I was pretty attached to it. I became fluent with computers at a small age. I taught myself how to code when I reached college and I've always been fascinated by the applications that works on the computer and how can it be altered to suit our needs.



The main stream Internet began when I was in 4th semester while pursuing my Computer Science Engineering from Hitkarini College of Engineering and Technology, Jabalpur, India. I have always been fascinated by the online communities and how people share and explore using the web, while I continue to teach myself web and online communication. This was a small glance into my life. And currently I work from home, I have made many small to medium projects for many different poeple.
